Calendars are also called as date pickers
How to Handle Calendar:
1. Click on the calendar
2. locate the month and get text
3. if the desired text is not visible --> we will keep clicking on and keep updating month text
4. as soon as we find our desired text ---> grab all the Cells from the table
5. loop through the cell
6. one the cells value is matched with our condition --> click on the matched date
